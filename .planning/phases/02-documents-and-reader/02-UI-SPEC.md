## State Contract

| State | Visual treatment | Open action | Required copy | Source |
|-------|------------------|-------------|---------------|--------|
| `uploading` | Card appears immediately at top of grid with spinner and accent progress fill | disabled | `Uploading PDF...` | `D-06`, research upload-session pattern |
| `processing` | Keep card visible with non-destructive progress badge and calm explanatory copy | disabled | `Preparing this document for reading...` | `D-06`, `D-07` |
| `ready` | Accent-emphasized ready badge, standard card actions enabled | enabled | `Ready to open` | `D-07` |
| `failed` | Destructive-tinted badge and inline help text inside the card | disabled | `This PDF couldn't be processed. Upload it again or delete it.` | `D-06`, phase default |

Do not remove non-ready documents from the library. Newest uploads stay first so lifecycle changes are visible without searching.

## Interaction Contract

| Flow | Contract | Source |
|------|----------|--------|
| Upload start | Clicking `Upload PDF` opens the file picker immediately. After selection, create the optimistic library card before transfer completes. | `DOC-01`, research upload-session pattern |
| Upload validation | Reject non-PDF files inline on the upload surface. Validation feedback must appear in-page, not toast-only. | `DOC-01`, research validation guidance |
| Upload completion | Transition from `uploading` to `processing` without removing the card. Swap any progress meter for status text once transfer completes. | `D-06`, `D-08` |
| Ready open | Only `ready` documents can open. `Open` is disabled for other states and must never navigate to a broken reader route. | `D-07`, `DOC-04` |
| Delete | Deleting from the card or reader uses the same confirmation dialog copy. On success, remove the document from the current view without a full-page reload. | `D-04`, `DOC-05` |
| Reader navigation | Previous/next page controls remain visible above the viewer. Disable previous on page 1 and next on the last page. | `READ-02` |
| Reader continuity | Returning from the reader goes back to `Library`, not `Dashboard`. Preserve document identity in the header at all times. | `D-16` |
| Future AI continuity | The companion panel must include a visually reserved AI area now, labeled as coming next, so Phase 3 slots in without a layout reset. | `D-14`, `READ-03` |

## Accessibility Contract

- All icon-only actions in the reader and mobile navigation must meet the `44px` minimum touch target.
- Status must be communicated by text plus icon/spinner, not color alone.
- The upload surface, delete dialog, and page controls must expose clear keyboard focus using the existing ring token.
- Page controls require accessible labels: `Previous page`, `Next page`, and `Page {n} of {total}`.
- Error messages must appear inline near the affected surface so they are visible without relying on toasts.

---

## Motion Contract

- Use the existing subtle hover lift on actionable cards and primary buttons; cap duration at `200ms` ease-out.
- Upload progress may animate linearly while bytes are moving, then settle immediately into the next lifecycle state.
- Avoid large panel slides or full-screen spinners; Phase 02 should feel calm and fast, not theatrical.
